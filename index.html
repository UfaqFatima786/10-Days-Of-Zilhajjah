The Blessed 10 Days of Dhul Hijjah

The first ten days of Dhul Hijjah are among the most sacred days in Islam. Increase your worship, duas, dhikr, and good deeds during these blessed days.

What is Dhul Hijjah?

Dhul Hijjah is the 12th and final month of the Islamic calendar. It is one of the holiest and most blessed months in Islam.

Why is it Important?

This sacred month includes the pilgrimage of Hajj and the celebration of Eid ul Adha. Muslims around the world increase worship, prayer, fasting, and good deeds during these days.

Why Are These Days Special?

The first 10 days of Dhul Hijjah are considered among the best days of the year. These days are filled with mercy, forgiveness, and opportunities to earn rewards from Allah.
